About Us

Medicare is a complex topic that can be confusing to most Americans turning 65. Our goal is to help educate by taking an overwhelming topic and boiling it down to easy to understand language.

The problem for most Americans turning 65 is that insurance companies are bombarding your mailbox each week, your phone is blowing up, and your email is full of spam.

We conduct complimentary Medicare seminars or a one on one Medicare consultations at no cost or obligation. We can then help save you time and money by helping you find the right plan for you. We keep you from making mistakes that can cost you thousands of dollars down the road.
